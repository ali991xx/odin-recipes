Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A delicious grilled cheese sandwich made in a pan

Ingredients

  • white sandwich bread
  • butter
  • thinly sliced cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Place one slice butter side down on a hot skillet
  3. Top with cheese and another slice of bread, butter side up
  4. Cook until bottom slice is brown, then flip
  5. Cook until the cheese has melted
