Cauliflower Cheese

Description

A delicious cauliflower cheese recipe that is perfect as a side dish with a roast dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 caulflower
  • 40 g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 450 milk
  • 130 g mature cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp English mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C, gas mark 6, fan 180C
  2. Half fill a pan with water and bring to the boil
  3. Remove the outer leaves from the cauliflower and chop into florets
  4. Add the florets to the pan and bring to the boil. Cook for 5 minutes, until the cauliflower is tender but firm
  5. Drain the cauliflower
  6. Make the sauce by melting the butter in a pan over a low heat the stirring in the flour until it forms a smooth paste
  7. Add the milk a little at a time, stirring until fully combined.
  8. Stir gently until it starts to thicken. Add the cheese and mustard and stir until it melts
  9. Remove sauce from heat
  10. Pour some of the sauce into an ovenproof dish. Add the cauliflower and pour over the rest of the sauce
  11. Grate 50 g cheddar on top
  12. Bake for 20 minutes until golden brown and bubbling
